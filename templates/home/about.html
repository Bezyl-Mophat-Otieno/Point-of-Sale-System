Bubble Bursts

Clean suds , delightfull scents

Welcome to Bubble Bursts, your one-stop-shop for all your cleaning needs! We are a company that specializes in producing high-quality detergents, including fabric softeners, soap, bleach, and detoxicants. Our mission is to provide our customers with effective and affordable cleaning solutions that make their lives easier. Located in Kisii County, specifically at Kisii University, we are proud to be a local business that serves the community. Our company was founded by Brian Muchiri Mugo, who saw a need for high-quality cleaning products that were safe for the environment and effective at removing tough stains. With his vision and leadership, Bubble Bursts has grown to become a trusted name in the cleaning industry. At Bubble Bursts, we believe that quality should never be compromised. That's why we use only the best ingredients and manufacturing processes to create our products. Our team of experts, led by our Chief Technical Officer, Bezyl Mophat Otieno, works tirelessly to develop and improve our formulas to ensure that they meet our high standards of performance and safety. We understand that every customer has different needs when it comes to cleaning. That's why we offer a wide range of products, from gentle fabric softeners to powerful bleach and detoxicants. Whether you're washing delicate fabrics or tackling tough stains, we have a solution that's right for you. At Bubble Bursts, we are committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. We use eco-friendly ingredients and packaging materials whenever possible, and we are constantly exploring new ways to reduce waste and conserve resources. Thank you for choosing Bubble Bursts as your trusted partner in clean. We are proud to serve the community of Kisii County and look forward to continuing to provide you with high-quality cleaning solutions for years to come.
